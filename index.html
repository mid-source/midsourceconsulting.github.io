About MidSource Consulting

MidSource Consulting began its journey in October 2019 as an innovative IT startup company in Kochi, Kerala, India. What started as a vision to offer language-agnostic full software testing services across multiple platforms and technologies has evolved into a comprehensive IT solutions provider.

Today, we are proud to be a team of highly skilled engineers, partnering with both national and international clients to deliver exceptional quality assurance and development services.
